Cybersecurity Awareness Dashboard

Your ultimate source for staying safe online!

Tip #1: Use strong passwords with a mix of letters, numbers, and special characters.

Tip #2: Enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) for accounts wherever possible.

Tip #3: Keep your operating system, browser, and software up-to-date with the latest patches.

Tip #4: Avoid connecting to public Wi-Fi without using a VPN.

Tip #5: Verify links and email addresses before clicking on them to prevent phishing attacks.

Tip #6: Back up your data regularly on external drives or secure cloud services.

Tip #7: Never share your passwords or save them in easily accessible locations.
